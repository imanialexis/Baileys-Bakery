Oatmeal Cranberry Cookies Recipe

under Simple Cookie Recipes

Ingredients
 
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1-1/2 cups sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 cups quick-cooking oats
  • 1 cup raisins
  • 1 cup coarsely chopped fresh or frozen cranberries
  • 1 tablespoon grated orange peel
  • 1 package (10 to 12 ounces) white baking chips
Cooking time: 25 mins Serving Size: 72 people

 

If you love cranberries and oatmeal cookies then this recipe will be a nice little treat for you because it’s time to bring them both together for some good old Oatmeal Cranberry Cookies!

These cookies are very easy to bake and will take you around 10-15 minutes prep and 10 minutes to cook. Once baked they look and taste DELICIOUS! Their also fun to bake with the kids 😀

Cooking Directions

  • Grab yourself a bowl and start creaming the butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Then add your eggs(one at a time) while beating them in nicely. Then add your vanilla and beat in to make a nice yummy mix.
  • Now it’s time to add the flour, baking powder, salt and baking soda to the creamed mixture and combine them all by mixing them together. 
  • Add and stir in the oats, raisins, cranberries and orange peel until mixed well.
  • Once done, stir in baking chips.
  • On a greased baking sheet use a rounded teaspoon to make dollops which will form into your cookies. Space them about 2inches appart.
  • Bake at 375° for 10-12 minutes or until the edges are lightly browned.
  • Leave them to cool on wire racks.

The end result should be a batch of nice browned cookies with dots of cranberry. The taste is kind of like a yummy chewy breakfast cookie, well that’s the best way I can describe it.

They make a great snack for kids or have them with your cuppa of a morning or afternoon. Every time I make a batch of these Oatmeal Cranberry Cookies they’re gone very fast! Our kids absolutely LOVE them..
